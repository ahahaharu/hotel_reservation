Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2025

ООО "LuxStay" (hereinafter referred to as "Hotel", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our guests, clients, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with Russian Federation legislation, including Federal Law No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data".

1. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, surname, email address, phone number, residential address.
  • Booking Data: Check-in and check-out dates, room preferences, special requests (e.g., staying with children or pets).
  • Payment Information: Bank card details or other payment methods (processed through secure payment systems).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies (with your consent).
  • Reviews and Feedback: Information provided in reviews, surveys, or when contacting our support team.

2. How We Collect Data

We collect data in the following ways:

  • Direct Submission: When you fill out booking forms, register on the website, leave reviews, or contact us.
  • Automated Collection: Through cookies and similar technologies when you visit grandhotel.ru.
  • From Third Parties: Through partners (e.g., booking aggregators) if you book a room via their platforms.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process and confirm room and service bookings.
  • Provide personalized services (e.g., accommodating your preferences).
  • Send notifications about bookings, promotions, and special offers (with your consent).
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • Analyze and improve the quality of our services and website.

4. How We Protect Your Data

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption during transmission (SSL/TLS).
  • Access restriction to authorized personnel only.
  • Storage on secure servers.
  • Regular updates to security systems.

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We may share your data with:

  • Partners providing booking or payment services (e.g., payment systems).
  • Government authorities when required by law.

We do not sell or share your data for marketing purposes without your consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. For example:

  • Booking data is retained for 5 years to comply with accounting requirements.
  • Technical data (cookies) is stored for the duration of the cookie or until you delete them.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to data processing.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (e.g., marketing emails).

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@luxstay.ru.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit website functionality.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy due to changes in legislation or our processes. The current version is always available at grandhotel.ru/privacy-policy/.

10. Contact Us
